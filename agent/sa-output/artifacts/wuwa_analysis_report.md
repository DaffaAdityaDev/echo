# Wuthering Waves 2025 Meta Analysis Report

## Executive Summary
This analysis provides comprehensive insights into the current meta for Wuthering Waves (Version 3.1) as of May 2025, focusing on optimal character builds and team compositions that maximize DPS and win rates across various game modes.

## Current Meta Overview
- **Elemental Priority:** Fire/Frost/Light combinations dominate
- **Core Strategy:** Fusion synergy maximization
- **Energy Management:** Critical for sustained performance

## Top 5 Best Teams Analysis

### Team 1: The Fiery Resonator
**Characters:**
1. Aemeath (Fusion Sword - Fire) - Core DPS
2. Ravine (Fusion Bow - Fire) - High burst damage  
3. Candia (Natural Elemental - Light) - Support/Healing

**Strengths:** Massive AoE fire damage, perfect for multi-enemy encounters.

### Team 2: The Frost Synergist
**Characters:**
1. Frostlands Resonator (Primary support)
2. Gloom (Fusion Sword - Frost) - Freezing damage
3. Raya (Natural Elemental - Light)

**Strengths:** Freeze mechanics + powerful single-target attacks.

### Team 3: The Ultimate Fusion
**Characters:**
1. Aemeath (5★ Fused)
2. Bard (Fusion Bow)
3. Sable (Natural Elemental)

**Strengths:** Highest combined fusion power, optimal for long battles.

### Team 4: The Light Weaver
**Characters:**
1. Candia (5★ Fusion)
2. Raya (5★ Fused) 
3. Twinblade (Fusion Sword)

**Strengths:** Light element synergy for area-of-effect healing and damage.

### Team 5: The Hybrid Dominator
**Characters:**
1. Aemeath (Core DPS)
2. Bard (Support rotation)
3. Ravine (Burst damage)
4. Candia (Healing)

**Strengths:** Most versatile for all game modes and difficulties.

## Performance Recommendations

### Optimal Build Components:
- **Weapon Set #634 (Twin Blades):** Best DPS performance
- **Echo 5 + Echo 9 Combo:** Maximum damage potential
- **Quick Skill Execution:** Essential for uninterrupted attacks

### Meta Evolution:
1. Prioritize Aemeath and Frostlands Resonator

## Conclusion
The current meta favors teams with strong elemental synergy (Fire/Frost/Light) and efficient fusion mechanics. Team compositions should prioritize character complementarity over individual power.
